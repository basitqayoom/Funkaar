In our daily lives, we see a tremendous decline in our Kashmiri culture and art all around us. Kashmiri culture is popular for its "Mosiki" which roughly translates to soulful music. From 'Waakh' of Lal ded to kalaam of 'Abdul Rashid Hafiz', the Kashmiri culture resonates in it all. It was around the 15th century when it all started and peaked until the end of the 20th century. The Kashmiri Mosiki has been regarded sacred by many and with utmost respect, We created this website to help revive our Kashmiri heritage in the form of instrumentals, full advantage of modern technology and the melodious sounds of the instruments have been integrated in a way that is simple and pleasing to the user eye. This website has been created while keeping in mind that the user base may vary from old people to the very young ones, that is why the user interface has been kept as simple as possible while getting most out of the technology used to build it. The fun and user-friendly experience is surely going to get you on your toes. We have tried to intermingle the old culture with something pleasing to a young user's ears so that he/she develops an interest in the old tradition. We encourage more and more people to come up with similar ideas like this. Since life is nothing but a giant learning curve, we are doing our part, we are still learning and more and more features are on the way to get you guys all excited.
